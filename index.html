How It's Made

From Milk to Golden Ghee

🐄
1
Milk Collection
Fresh A2 milk collected daily from indigenous Indian cows raised on natural fodder at trusted partner farms.
🥛
2
Curd & Churning
Milk is set into curd and slow-churned using traditional Bilona method to extract pure white butter.
🔥
3
Slow Cooking
Butter is simmered on low flame until water evaporates, leaving pure golden ghee with a signature aroma.
📦
4
Pack & Deliver
Quality tested, hygienically packed in airtight tins, and dispatched with care to your doorstep across India.
